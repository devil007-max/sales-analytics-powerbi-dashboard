üß† Business Insights (Summary)

- **Technology** category leads in both Sales and Profit.
- **Furniture** has high Sales but relatively low Profit ‚Üí suggests cost/discount issues.
- **West region** is the top revenue contributor, while Central & South need focus.
- **Consumer segment** is the largest revenue driver; **Corporate** shows strong profit potential.
- **Standard Class** is the most used and most profitable shipping mode.
- Higher discounts are often associated with **lower profit**, especially in some regions and categories.
- Forecasts indicate continued business growth with opportunities to optimize pricing and shipping.
